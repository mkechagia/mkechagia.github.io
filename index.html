Biographical Information

I am a Research Fellow at CREST, in the Department of Computer Science of University College London (UCL). My research supervisors are Dr. Federica Sarro and Professor Mark Harman. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Delft University of Technology and a member of the Software Engineering Research Group under the supervision of Dr. Georgios Gousios. I finished my PhD in Software Engineering in the Department of Management Science and Technology, at the Athens University of Economics and Business, under the supervision of Prof. Diomidis Spinellis. Before that, I pursued my MSc in Computing (Software Engineering) at Imperial College London.

Research

My research interests lie in the areas of software engineering, software verification, crash data analytics, and programming languages. Through my research, I wish to combine software engineering and formal techniques for building robust software systems and increasing programmer productivity.
